# Climate Policy Event Study

## Research Question
How do major climate policy announcements affect green and brown stock returns?

## Methodology
**Language:** Python  
**Methods:** Event study — market model, CAR analysis

## Data
Yahoo Finance, 9 hand-collected climate policy events (2021–2024)

## Key Findings
Green stocks show positive abnormal returns around pro-climate policies; fossil fuel stocks respond asymmetrically.
